FAQ - Blood Management System

1. What is the Blood Management System?

The Blood Management System is a web-based platform designed to facilitate the management of blood donations, storage, and distribution.

2. How do I register as a blood donor?

To register as a blood donor, please visit the registration page and fill out the required information. You will be contacted by our team for further verification.

3. Can I request blood donations through the system?

Yes, registered users can submit requests for blood donations based on their specific requirements. Our system will match your request with suitable donors and notify you accordingly.

4. Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take the privacy and security of your personal information seriously. Our system employs robust security measures to safeguard your data.
